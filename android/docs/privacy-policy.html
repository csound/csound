Csound for Android - Privacy Policy

Csound for Android is a port of the free, open source programmable software sound synthesizer Csound to the Android operating system.

Csound for Android can, at the user's instruction, record audio or other data to a device's storage. However, neither this audio, nor any other data, personal or otherwise, produced by Csound for Android is collected, transmitted, stored, or used by Irreducible Productions, the Csound developers, or any other party.

Csound for Android does not provide in-app purchases.

Csound for Android does not display ads.

Csound for Android does not collect or transmit any usage statistics or software diagnostics.

Changes to this Privacy Policy will be communicated by updating this Policy on the Play Store and in the latest version of the Csound for Android app.

If you need to contact us, email us at: CSOUND@LISTSERV.HEANET.IE
